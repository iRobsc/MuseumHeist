GAME

Game Description:

The main goal of the game is to steal an artifact in a museum without getting caught and by solving puzzles.

It is a top down stealth game with a pixelated design. It is a single player game where the thief has to avoid the guards in order to not be caught. Strategies must be used to avoid different mechanics such as lasers.

To win a level, the thief has to steal the artifact and go out with it.

Plot:

One day, a 40-year-old man married and who has one kid wakes up and finds that his life has become boring. He has become accustomed to a routine and no longer feels like he is living new experiences and enjoying life. On that day, a school trip is planned by his son's school to the National Museum in Stockholm. He is responsible for being an accompanist. During the visit, he observes the different works of art and he hears the children in the class allude to a series: Casa de papel. He remembers seeing the first season more than a year ago and thinking back, an idea comes to him: "I'm going to become a thief !” He therefore inquired about the various museums and their security systems and decided to rob an object in each museum.
